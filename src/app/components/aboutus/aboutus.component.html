Founder:

Neeraj is the Founder and CEO at SUGARMATHS™.

Neeraj successfully launched a Web Products company, Trendyworks, in 2005 that went on to have thousands of customers across the Globe in a short period of time with a multi-million dollar turnover. Trendyworks has been recognised by NASSCOM as one of the top emerging product companies from India. Neeraj Holds a degree in Aerospace engineering from IIT Kharagpur.

SUGARMATHS™ is borne out of Neeraj's passion for education and a desire to create a technology platform that has a positive impact on the way teaching and learning happens today. SUGARMATHS™'s team consists of forward thinking Educationists, accomplished Academicians, talented software developers and extremely creative digital artists. Backed by this motivated team SUGARMATHS™ is set to revolutionize the school level education in India, at the student, teacher and school level.
